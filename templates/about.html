About Zampa

Dog care, the neighbourly way

Zampa is a community platform for dog owners in Barcelona to exchange walking and sitting services — based on mutual trust and neighbourhood proximity.

The idea

Inspired by HomeExchange — the idea is simple: I walk your dog this morning, you walk mine tonight. No money changes hands. Just neighbours helping neighbours.

We believe cities are more liveable when people know their neighbours. Zampa uses your dog as the perfect icebreaker.

How tokens work

Can't find someone to exchange with right now? You can still help a neighbour — and earn Zampa tokens for every walk or sit you provide without a direct exchange.

When you need care for your dog, spend your tokens. The token system makes it possible to participate even when schedules don't align.

Token values and full system coming soon.

Built for Barcelona

Zampa is built neighbourhood by neighbourhood — Poblenou, Gràcia, Eixample, Barceloneta, and beyond. The closer you are, the easier the exchange.
